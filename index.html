Why Hugin

Three reasons it works.

01

AI-first authoring

Claude writes the driver, you review. Every accepted driver becomes reference material for the next one. The library compounds.

02

Local-first

Full LAN access on your machine. Probe Modbus TCP, MQTT, HTTP on your own network. No agent to install. No cloud-dependency for the work that matters.

03

Open by default

Every driver ships as a PR against frahlg/forty-two-watts. Your name on the commit. No lock-in.
